Weekly Schedule
Monday · Wednesday · Friday
CurrentBody LED Mask
10 mins after serums — wait 2 mins for absorption. Always before moisturiser. Rest days between sessions are not optional — skin needs 24–48h to complete the repair cycle. Never on consecutive nights.
Tuesday · Friday
Xioimy Dissolving Microneedle
After cleanse + tone, before serums. Press firmly 30 secs, leave overnight. Niacinamide + Vit C dissolve directly into skin. Follow with Ceramidin only — no other actives. Never same night as LED.
Wednesday
Eelhoe Dark Spot Patches
Press directly onto cheek pigmentation patches after serums. Leave overnight. Non-needling night — safe to layer with serums.
Thursday
CurrentBody Hydrogel Mask
Apply hydrogel for 15 mins, remove, then continue with serums and moisturiser. No LED tonight — Thursday is a dedicated hydration recovery night between Wednesday and Friday's LED session.
Sunday
Rest or Xioimy Bio-Collagen Film
Recovery night. Optionally apply Bio-Collagen Film to forehead + cheeks. Press firmly and leave to dissolve. Use as targeted hydration treatment only.
⚠ Critical Rules — Never Break
LED mask on Mon · Wed · Fri only — never consecutive nights, rest days are essential
Never use Xioimy microneedle same night as LED mask
Never microneedle two nights in a row
Always SPF50 the morning after any needling
No strong actives (retinoids) for 48h post-needling
