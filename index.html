Weekly Treatments
Mon · Wed · Fri · Sun
CurrentBody LED Mask
10 mins after serums. Always before moisturiser. Skip night after any microneedling.
Tue · Fri
Xioimy Dissolving Microneedle
Cleansed dry skin. Press firmly 30 secs, leave overnight. Delivers niacinamide + Vit C directly into skin — your current best pigmentation tool. Follow with Ceramidin only. Do NOT use same night as LED mask.
Wednesday or Thursday
Eelhoe Dark Spot Patches
Press directly on cheek pigmentation patches. Leave overnight. Non-needling nights only.
Thursday
CurrentBody Hydrogel Mask + LED
Apply hydrogel 15 mins first, remove, then do LED session. Maximum hydration + light therapy combo.
Sunday
Rest or Bio-Collagen Film
Let skin recover. Optionally use Xioimy Bio-Collagen Film on forehead + cheeks as a hydration treat. Press firmly and leave to dissolve. No needling — this is your skin's recovery night.
Every Other Sunday
Xioimy Bio-Collagen Film
Forehead + cheeks. Press firmly and leave to dissolve. Use as hydration treatment only — topical collagen films cannot structurally replace collagen.
⚠ Critical Rules — Never Break
Never microneedle two nights in a row — Tue + Fri max
Never use Xioimy microneedle same night as LED mask
Always SPF50 the morning after any needling
No strong actives (Vit C, retinoids) for 48h post-needling
